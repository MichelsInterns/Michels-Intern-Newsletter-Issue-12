COMPANY SPOTLIGHT
What does Michels Trenchless GmbH do?
Ever wonder how we install massive pipelines without digging huge trenches? 

Thats what our Trenchless team in Germany does best. Using advanced techniques like Direct Pipe  and horizontal directional drilling (HDD), they create underground pathways for pipelines with minimal surface (and environmental) disruptions.

Innovation, precision, and craftsmanship, all rolled into one! 
Project Profile: Offenbach 110-kV HDD
Michels Trenchless GmbH completed a 300-meter-long HDD in the inner city of Offenbach, Germany. Ground conditions that included clay with bands of limestone made drilling difficult, but the crews skills and determination overcame the challenges.

A bundle of eight HDPE pipes were pulled in the final 91.44-centimeter borehole. These pipes will soon accommodate new 110-kV power lines.
